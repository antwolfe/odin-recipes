Protein Fluff

Description

This protein fluff can be customized to any flavor and only needs 4 ingredients!

Try topping this delicious dessert with fresh fruit or other toppings for a truly yummy treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of fresh or frozen fruit (ex: Strawberries)
  • 1/2 cup milk or nut milk
  • 1 scoop protein powder (vanilla works all around in flavor, but any will work)
  • 1 tsp xantham gum

Steps

  1. Blend the fruit and milk in a blender until slushy
  2. Add in protein powder and xantham gum, blend until thick (about 1 minute)
  3. Serve
